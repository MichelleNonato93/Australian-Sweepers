ASC COMMERCIAL CLEANING EQUIPMENT

Australia's Leading Sweeping and Scrubbing Company

ASC is Australia's leading industrial and commercial floor cleaning equipment company. With over 15 years of cleaning experience, ASC Pty Ltd (Australian Sweeper Company) leads the way in offering a comprehensive range of professional floor sweepers and scrubbers to suit all industries in Australia and New Zealand. Our commercial and industrial sweeper and scrubber machines are sourced and tested for effective cleaning performance and lasting quality and are backed by our industry leading warranties.

Our extensive range includes large heavy duty industrial sweepers and scrubbers, street sweepers and scrubber driers and a wide range of small push behind manual sweepers, motorised electric sweepers and scrubbers. We also offer alternative fuel choices such as LPG, CNG (compressed natural gas), diesel power as well as traction battery.

ASC not only leads the way in selling new sweepers and scrubbers but provides nationwide 24 hour support, advice and service for your convenience. ASC imports and sells the leading international sweeping and scrubbing equipment, including Dulevo International, Eureka, Mach Line and Factory Cat machines. By offering the widest range of quality built leading cleaning brands to suit all industries, we are able to bring to the Australian commercial and industrial markets the world's best sweeper and scrubber machine technologies available on the global market today. We are not restricted to one brand or manufacturer; we sell several of the world's best floor cleaning machines, ensuring we can always offer the right equipment for every situation.
